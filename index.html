Introduction

Michelle Liu is a violinist who was trained by a full-time music specialty talent center since 7 years old and graduated from a music college. She was a professor at a college and the first violinist at the Shenzhen symphony. She accumulated a vast amount of experience from teaching students. Some of them award-winning and become violinists. She is very successful at the education field.
